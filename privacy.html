Privacy

How registration information is used.

SunnySide only collects the details needed to manage registration, communicate with families, and support a safe camp day.

Information We Collect

Registration details

Family contact details

SunnySide collects parent names, email addresses, phone numbers, emergency contacts, and any notes shared during registration.

Camper details

SunnySide collects each child's name, age, camp selection, and optional allergy or support notes provided by the family.

Payment records

Payments are processed through Stripe. SunnySide stores limited payment confirmation details needed to match payments to registrations.

How Information Is Used

Camp operations only

Communication

Contact details are used to confirm registrations, answer questions, and share camp reminders or updates.

Safety and planning

Health notes, emergency contacts, and child details are used only to help staff prepare and support campers appropriately.

Internal records

Registration and payment details are stored for scheduling, attendance planning, and payment reconciliation.

Sharing and Storage

Limited use of your information

No routine resale or marketing list sharing

SunnySide does not sell registration information or share it for unrelated marketing purposes.

Service providers

Registration information may be stored using tools that support camp operations, including Google Sheets and Stripe.

Questions or corrections

If you need to update your information or have privacy questions, contact sunnysidesummercamper@gmail.com.
