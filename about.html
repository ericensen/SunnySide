Our Story

Meet Natalie and Alexa

We're Natalie Christensen and Alexa White, and we started SunnySide Summer Camp to create the kind of camp kids actually look forward to. SunnySide is a fun, high-energy summer camp created for kids, by kids, with adult supervision every step of the way.

Our focus is on active outdoor play, creative hands-on activities, teamwork, and real friendships. Everything is supported by responsible adult supervision to make sure the environment is safe, organized, and welcoming.

We wanted to create a place where kids can unplug, try new things, build confidence, and enjoy their summer right here in Draper, Utah. Families choose SunnySide because it combines kid-designed fun with a safe, structured setting, a small-group feel, and an experience that is simple and affordable.
